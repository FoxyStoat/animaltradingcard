  • Scientific Name: Vulpes vulpes
  • Average Life Span: 18 months to 2
  • Weight: Dogs 5.9kg, Vixens 5.2kg
  • Diet: Omnivores

Beautiful Red Foxes have elongated bodies and relatively short limbs, there fluffy bushy tails are longer than half of the length of their body. Red foxes are usually together in pairs or small groups consisting of families. The males are called dogs, the females are vixens and the cubs are known as kits The red fox is distinguished from other fox species by its ability to adapt quickly to new environments and out of all the species of foxes, the red species is more likely to become urbanised.
